Description

This site has been created to allow players of Hell Let Loose to find different clans which play the game that we all love and enjoy, many players find after they have played the game for some months, that they are looking for something more organised or competitive. This normally leads you to wanting to look for a clan to join, but where do you look and who do you join. Well through this site we hope to answer both those questions.

You will find on the homepage below a full A-Z list of many of the different clans which play the game each with a clickable link. On each clan page you will have the same basic layout. It will include the name of the clan, clan logo, description of the clan, discord link, website link (if they have one) and any upcoming games they have organised to play. All of which will allow you to browser around the different clans to get a feel of what suites you best and maybe get in contact with the later down the line.

On the other hand, if you have found this site and you are an owner of a clan or have a high position in the clan then there is something for your as well. By creating an account on the site and ticketing the option of clan representative in the registration form you will be provided with some extra features. As a Clan Representative you will have the ability to create your own clan page for your clan as long as the name is unique, and you have not already created a clan. Along with that you may want to organise games against other clans, well we can help you there as well. Once logged in you will find a match request link which will take you to a match request form that once filled in correctly will send a game request to another clan representative.

As a logged in user, you will also find that the navigation bar will update to show the notifications link, here you will be able to find any match requests that you have sent and any that you have received along with a button to view the full details. For any games that you have been sent you will have the option to either accept or decline the game request and accepted games appear on your clan page. For all logged in users they will also find in the notifications tab a link to raise an admin ticket, meaning that if you find any issues or need help, please raise a ticket and then admin will investigate this and from the status you will be able to tell the progress of your request.
