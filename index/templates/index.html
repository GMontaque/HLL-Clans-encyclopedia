Description

Welcome to the Hell Let Loose Encyclopaedia. This site has been created to allow players of Hell Let Loose to find different clans with which to play the game that we all love and enjoy. Many players find, after having played the game for some months, that they are looking for something more organised or competitive. This normally leads to the player wanting to join a clan, but where do you look and who do you join? Well, through this site, we hope to answer both of those questions.

Below is a list of the many different clans which play the game. They are listed from A-Z and each has a clickable link. Each clan page has the same basic layout. It includes the clan name, clan logo, description of the clan, discord link, website link (if they have one) and any upcoming games they have organised to play. This is all you need to browse around the different clans and get a feel of what suites you best and maybe get in contact with them later down the line.

Clan Owners : If you have stumbled across this site and you are the owner of a clan or have a high position in a clan, consider yourself invited to add your clan to our list. By creating an account on the site and ticking the option of ‘Clan Representative’ in the registration form, you will be provided with some extra features. As a Clan Representative you have the ability to create your own clan page for your clan as long as the name is unique, and you have not already created a clan. We can also help to organise games against other clans.

Once logged in, you will find a ‘match request link’ which will take you to a match request form. Complete this to send a game request to another clan representative. Provided you are logged in, you can check progress of your game requests in the notifications link on the navigation bar. Click the link to view all match requests sent or received. Each of the requests is provided with a button to view the full details. For any games requests received, you have the option to either accept or decline and accepted games appear on your clan page. A link to raise an admin ticket is provided on the notifications page. If you are experiencing any issues or need help, please raise a ticket for the admin team who will investigate. The ‘status’ of your ticket will keep you informed of its progress .
